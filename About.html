About

Born in Brazil, to a lively Italian family, I grew up tinkering with robotics. With an interest in innovation, I decided to expand my knowledge in the field by studying Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. After working in several innovation departments, I joined a content development with UC Berkeley to gain experience in ideation and storytelling. Currently working at Xpanse AI, a predictive modelling startup, I am developing instructive videos in data science. After this experience, I hope to leverage my robotics and content skills to be an integral part of an innovation and development team.
