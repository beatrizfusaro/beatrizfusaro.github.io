Engineer

A maker since childhood, creating is my true passion. As an engineer, I have worked in the design and execution of a range of robotics projects. From coding to machining, check out some highlights.

Agricultural Robot for Monsanto

To travel through crops and find weed and diseases hotspots, I employed stochastic navigation and computer vision to build a fully automated agricultural robot.

Fruit Picker with NIRS for GTRI

The challenge was to pick ripe fruits using an autonomous robot. I designed a soft robotics claw equipped with a near infrared spectroscopy device, that was capable of determining how ripe the fruit was and picking it without bruising.

SumoBot for Robojackets

To participate in the All Japan Robot Sumo Tournament, I applied concepts of machine design and trajectory prediction/automation, designing and building an automated sumo robot.
