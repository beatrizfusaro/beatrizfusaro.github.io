Researcher

From my work in several high tech companies and labs, I have gathered a lot of research experience, especially in the medical and robotics fields. Here are some of my research spotlights.

Medical Image Visualization for Siemens

Since an MRI image is visualized based on contrast, it is hard to identify tumors and bleeds. I designed a new visualization algorithm based on concrete tissue values that allows for the immediate identification of tumors and bleeds. The algorithm was published in a patent.

Deblurring for Biorobotics Lab

The challenge was to reduce motion blur in high speed systems. I designed a mechanical rig capable of controlled motion. The motion pattern reduces the blur mechanically, making it easier for deblurring algorithms to perfect the image.
