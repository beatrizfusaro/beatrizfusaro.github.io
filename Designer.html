I have always loved the fine arts. After completing an apprenticeship to an art studio, I decided to focus on my art skills practical areas for the tech industry. With that in mind, I worked in projects involving animation and UX design. Here are some highlights.

Webdesign and Social Media Campaign for WRC

Georgia Tech's Women's Resource Center lacked campus awareness. To change that, I was tasked with revamping the department's website and developing a new social media plan. With the suggested changes, the website traffic increased by 200% in one month.

UI Design for SCBA Monitoring

Oxygen tanks used by firefighters lack good methods to monitor the remaining air time. For a device that can be attached to an SCBA system to monitor the oxygen levels, I designed an app capable of relaying the information to the commander.
